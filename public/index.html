Help & Features
Spaced Repetition

Cards are scheduled based on your performance. After answering, choose:

  • Again - Reset progress (review in 1 day)
  • Hard - Shorter interval than normal
  • Good - Standard interval increase
  • Easy - Longer interval than normal
Keyboard Shortcuts
  • Right Arrow - Next flashcard
  • Left Arrow - Previous flashcard
  • Space - Show/hide answer
  • 1 - Again
  • 2 - Hard
  • 3 - Good
  • 4 - Easy
Tagging System

Organize your flashcards by adding tags. Filter your cards by specific tags for targeted study.

Editing & Customization

Edit individual cards or use batch editing to modify multiple cards at once. Customize the app appearance and study settings.

Sharing

Share your flashcard sets with others by generating a direct link. Collaborate with classmates or friends.

Statistics

Track your learning progress with detailed statistics and a heatmap of your study activity.
